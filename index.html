01 — Our Story

A table built on three generations of fire, patience and hand-ground spice.

Since 1962

What began as a six-seat tiffin counter on Mount Road has, over six decades, become a quiet institution. We still grind our masalas at dawn. We still temper mustard in iron kadais. And we still believe that the best meal is the one cooked unhurriedly.

Our kitchen draws from the rivers of Chettinad, the coasts of Mangalore, the courtyards of old Madras — translated for a table that is contemporary, considered, and deeply rooted.
